Persona 1 (Primary)

Gender: Female
Location: Lives in University Commons (apartment on campus; closest to academic side but further away from NTID)
Status: Single
Devices: iPhone, Mac
Social Media: Instagram, Snapchat, Facebook - all for personal use.

About:

I am an undergraduate senior at RIT. I am deaf and communicate using American Sign Language (ASL). I am tech-savvy and use my phone all the time.

Motivations

Motivations for using the app - why would they use it: As a NTID student, most of my classes are on the opposite side of campus but I live in the University Commons (UC) and it takes me about 15 minutes to walk to the NTID side. I often stop at the College Grind to get coffee because it is closer to my classes. However, sometimes I stop at Midnight Oil because I like their drinks better. I really love their caramel Macchiato iced coffee or cool lime refresher with a bagel and lots of extra cream cheese.I only stop there if I have enough time because in the mornings the lines to order are really long. This app would allow me to easily order my drinks ahead of time so I can stop by, grab my drink and go rather than waiting in a 10-minute line. I hope I can pay with dining dollars or my debit card. The app would make ordering my drink easier because I won’t have to communicate with the cashier, most of the cashiers do not know ASL. In case I get there before my drink is ready, I would really like the app to notify me when my order is ready. I hope the drinks are clearly labeled so I know which one is mine.

Goals

  • Wants to see a full menu (text and images) and be able to order food and drinks
  • Customize food and drink order (leave comments)
  • Needs to see an ETA on her order to plan her trip
  • Set a pick-up time
  • Needs a notification when her order is ready
  • Option to pay with dining dollars
  • Reward system, getting the 10th coffee free
